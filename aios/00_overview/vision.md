## Current Focus Period

**Q2 2026 — DisptchMama MVP Development & Operational Integration**

This phase is focused on building and deploying the first functional version of DisptchMama into live GS Retrofit scheduling workflows.

## Strategic Priorities

### 1. Build a Functional Dispatch System (MVP)
Create a working scheduling and dispatch platform that replaces or significantly reduces reliance on manual coordination.

### 2. Reduce Scheduling Time & Cognitive Load
Minimize the time and mental effort required by Christian to schedule, adjust, and manage daily inspections.

### 3. Enable Real-Time Scheduling Flexibility
Design a system that supports constant schedule changes, including drag-and-drop reassignment, time adjustments, and dynamic updates across all views.

### 4. Establish Scheduling Logic & Rules Engine
Define and implement consistent logic for:
- Inspector assignment
- Time estimation
- Route efficiency
- Workload balancing

### 5. Lay Foundation for AI-Assisted Dispatch
Begin introducing intelligent recommendations for:
- Best time slots
- Optimal inspector assignment
- Conflict detection and resolution

## What Success Looks Like

- Christian can manage a full day of scheduling in significantly less time (target: <2 hours/day)
- Majority of jobs are assigned quickly with minimal back-and-forth
- Schedule changes can be handled in seconds, not minutes
- Inspector workload is balanced and visible at all times
- Unassigned jobs are reduced to near zero
- The system becomes the primary interface for scheduling, not a secondary tool

## Key Decisions & Open Questions

### Manual vs. Automated Control
How much control should remain with the scheduler vs. being automated by the system?

### Level of AI Involvement
Should DisptchMama act as:
- A recommendation engine (assistant), or
- A decision-making engine (autonomous dispatch)?

### Scheduling Interface Design
What is the most intuitive UI for rapid scheduling?
- Timeline-based (calendar view)?
- Queue + assignment model?
- Hybrid system?

### Handling Scheduling Volatility
What is the best system design to handle frequent changes without breaking workflow continuity?

### Integration with Existing Systems
How tightly should DisptchMama integrate with:
- Seller's Compliance platform
- Invoicing and revenue tracking
- Customer intake systems
