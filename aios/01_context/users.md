# Users & Roles

> Adapted for Seller’s Compliance — based on GS Retrofit operational workflow.

## Primary User

### Scheduling & Dispatch Coordinator

**Role**: Scheduler / Dispatch Coordinator — Seller’s Compliance

The Scheduling & Dispatch Coordinator is responsible for managing and coordinating all incoming inspection and work completion requests.

This role operates at the center of the business, ensuring that jobs are scheduled efficiently while balancing:

* Customer preferences
* Inspector availability
* Geographic routing
* Real-time operational changes

The coordinator acts as the primary decision-maker for:

* Appointment timing
* Inspector assignment
* Schedule adjustments throughout the day

This role exists in a **high-volume, real-time environment** where speed, accuracy, and adaptability are critical to maintaining smooth operations.

### Key Responsibilities

* **Schedule Appointments**
  Coordinate and confirm inspection and work completion appointments based on availability and customer needs.

* **Dispatch Inspectors**
  Assign jobs to inspectors based on location, workload, and efficiency.

* **Manage Schedule Changes**
  Handle reschedules, cancellations, and same-day adjustments in real time.

* **Optimize Routes & Workload**
  Ensure inspectors are efficiently scheduled with minimal downtime and logical routing.

* **Coordinate Communication**
  Act as the central point of contact for scheduling updates between customers, inspectors, and internal operations.

## Field Users

### Inspector

**Role**: Field Technician — Seller’s Compliance

Inspectors are responsible for executing jobs in the field, including both inspections and work completion.

They operate on mobile devices and rely on the system for daily assignments and job details.

### Key Responsibilities

* Perform compliance inspections (10–15 minute walkthroughs)
* Identify and document compliance issues
* Complete on-site work when authorized
* Upload notes, photos, and results
* Update job status in real time
* Communicate field conditions back to dispatch when needed

---

## Administrative Users

### Admin

**Role**: Operations Oversight — Seller’s Compliance

Admin users have full visibility and control over the system and are responsible for monitoring operations and performance.

### Key Responsibilities

* Oversee scheduling and dispatch operations
* Monitor Command Center metrics (jobs, workload, revenue)
* Resolve scheduling conflicts or escalations
* Manage users and system settings
* Ensure operational consistency and quality

  Provide clear visibility and structured workflows to minimize manual thinking.
